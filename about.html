Project Background

项目背景

The Feixing All-sky Transient Exploration (FATE) project was initiated in 2025, led by Chinese astronomy enthusiasts. With the rapid development of multi-band astronomical observation technology, traditional single-band survey methods can no longer meet the needs of detecting complex cosmic transient events. FATE aims to fill this gap by integrating data from optical, radio, X-ray, and gamma-ray telescopes to achieve comprehensive all-sky transient detection.

飞星全天区瞬变源巡天探测计划（FATE）于2025年启动，由中国天文爱好者发起。 随着多波段天文观测技术的飞速发展，传统单波段巡天方式已无法满足探测复杂宇宙瞬变事件的需求。 FATE 旨在填补这一空白，整合光学、射电、X射线和伽马射线望远镜的数据，实现全方位的全天区瞬变源探测。

Project Team

项目团队

The team acquires multi-dimensional astronomical data to ensure the accuracy and comprehensiveness of research results. The core team has rich experience in cosmic transient detection.

团队获取多维度天文数据，确保研究成果的准确性和全面性。 核心团队在宇宙瞬变源探测领域拥有丰富经验。
