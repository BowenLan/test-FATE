Mission & Goals

使命与目标

Uncovering the universe's hidden transient events

揭开宇宙中隐藏的瞬变事件

Core Mission

核心使命

FATE's core mission is to build a comprehensive, real-time, and high-precision all-sky transient event monitoring system, promote the discovery of new types of cosmic transient phenomena, and deepen humanity's understanding of the extreme physical processes in the universe. We are committed to making FATE a global platform for transient astronomy research, facilitating international cooperation and knowledge sharing.

FATE 的核心使命是构建一套全面、实时、高精度的全天区瞬变事件监测系统， 推动新型宇宙瞬变现象的发现，深化人类对宇宙极端物理过程的理解。 我们致力于将 FATE 打造为全球瞬变天文研究的平台，促进国际合作与知识共享。

Short-term Goals (2024-2027)

短期目标（2024-2027）

1. Complete the construction of the multi-band data integration platform and realize real-time processing of 80% of the global public astronomical data.
2. Develop and validate the intelligent event detection algorithm, achieving a detection accuracy of over 95% for known transient event types.

1. 完成多波段数据整合平台搭建，实现全球80%公开天文数据的实时处理。
2. 研发并验证智能事件检测算法，对已知瞬变事件类型的检测准确率达到95%以上。

Long-term Goals (2028-2035)

长期目标（2028-2035）

1. Discover at least 5 new types of cosmic transient events and publish breakthrough research results in top scientific journals.
2. Build a standardized data and algorithm sharing platform to promote the development of the global transient astronomy community.

1. 发现至少5种新型宇宙瞬变事件，在顶级科学期刊发表突破性研究成果。
2. 搭建标准化的数据与算法共享平台，推动全球瞬变天文领域的发展。
