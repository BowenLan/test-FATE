About The Project

关于项目

Feixing All-sky Transient Exploration (FATE) is a sky survey project based on multi-band data and information fusion technology, dedicated to searching for cosmic astronomical events.

飞星全天区瞬变源巡天探测计划（FATE）是一项基于多波段数据与信息融合技术的巡天项目，致力于搜寻宇宙中的各类天文瞬变事件。

Mission & Goal

使命与目标

Focus on the detection and research of cosmic astronomical transient events, explore the mysteries of the universe through advanced data processing and fusion technology.

专注于宇宙天文瞬变事件的探测与研究，通过先进的数据处理与融合技术，探索宇宙奥秘。
